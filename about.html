Our story

Built by someone who was
tired of settling.

Velai started with a simple frustration: fragrance, one of the most personal things you wear, was still a guessing game. We decided to fix that.

Allen spent his career as a management consultant, working across strategy and financial advisory at Monitor Deloitte, Kearney, and Jane Street. Analytical by profession, obsessive about scent by nature.

The problem was always the same. He'd walk into a department store, spray ten things on his arm, leave overwhelmed, and usually buy something familiar just to escape the experience. Then the bottle would sit on a shelf, slightly wrong, slightly disappointing. Too expensive to ignore, too mediocre to love.

The online alternatives weren't better. Generic quiz tools gave generic answers. Niche houses charged eye-watering prices for bottles you couldn't try first. And none of them used the signals that actually predict taste: your personality, your lifestyle, the music you listen to, the aesthetic you follow.

So he stopped looking for the right fragrance and started building it. He called Zak. They started sketching out what a properly data-driven, truly personalised fragrance brand could look like. Within weeks, the team had grown to include Andra and Maria, and Velai was born.

The brief was simple: make fragrance feel like it was made for you, because it actually was. Crafted by Guerlain-trained perfumers in France. Matched by AI. Delivered to your door at a price that doesn't require justification.

"I was fed up with paying luxury prices for something that never quite felt like mine."

Allen Ilica, Cofounder & CEO
