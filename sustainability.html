Ingredient sourcing

Grasse. Our atelier.
Givaudan Orpur.

Every Velai fragrance is composed and produced in Grasse, in the south of France, the birthplace of modern perfumery, home to the finest raw materials in the world. Our small atelier sits at the heart of this tradition, working with master perfumers who have spent careers in this landscape.

Our ingredient standard is Givaudan's Orpur programme, the most rigorous traceability and quality certification in the fragrance industry. Every raw material we source carries a full chain of custody: from the farm or distillery, through to the ingredient supplier, through to us. You know exactly what you're wearing, and where it came from.

Orpur ingredients are tested not just for purity but for socio-environmental impact across their entire supply chain. Farmers are paid fairly. Extraction methods are audited. The communities that grow our ingredients benefit from the relationship, not just the buyer at the end of it.

The science

Natural isn't always
better for your skin.

One of the most persistent myths in fragrance is that "natural" means safe, and "synthetic" means harmful. The evidence says otherwise, and we think you deserve to know the difference.

Citrus essential oils, beloved for their fresh, clean profile, are among the most common causes of skin sensitisation and phototoxicity in conventional perfumery. Cold-pressed bergamot, lemon, and lime oil contain furanocoumarins that can cause lasting pigmentation changes when skin is exposed to UV light after application. Some natural oakmoss and tree-moss extracts carry atranol, a potent allergen regulated by the IFRA for exactly this reason.

At Velai, we use the strongest, most skin-friendly variants available, including ISO-certified aroma chemicals and precisely dosed naturals that deliver the same olfactory experience without the sensitisation risk. Where a competitor like FFern leads with "100% natural" as a virtue, we lead with what the science actually supports: the right ingredient, in the right concentration, tested for your skin.

This isn't a compromise on quality. It's what quality actually means.

Packaging

Nothing wasted.
Everything considered.

We designed our packaging around one rule: if it can't be reused, recycled, or responsibly sourced, it doesn't belong in a Velai box.

01 ◇

Leather vial holders

Your Discovery Kit vials sit in individually stitched leather cradles, made by local artisans in the Grasse region. Vegetable-tanned, durable, and designed to last for years, not to be thrown away after one unboxing. This is your keepsake, not your waste.

02 ◯

Recycled glass throughout

Every vial, every bottle we produce is made from recycled glass, the most inert and recyclable packaging material available. Our full 50ml bottles are designed to be refilled indefinitely. When a glass vial reaches end of life, it goes back into the recycling stream without degradation in quality.

03 ▲

Minimal, recyclable outer packaging

Our shipping materials are FSC-certified card and recycled paper with no single-use plastics and no foam inserts. The outer box is printed with soy-based inks and is fully recyclable kerbside. We removed every element that served aesthetics over the environment.

Where we make it

Proudly manufacturing
in the European Union.

Every Velai fragrance is produced in France, subject to the most stringent environmental and labour regulations in the world. EU manufacturing means clean production standards, fair wages, and no race to the bottom on costs.

Our atelier in Grasse employs locally. The people who compound your fragrance are from the region, many with family ties to the perfumery industry that stretch back generations. We pay above the local market rate and we don't use agency workers or seasonal labour to keep costs down.

This is a deliberate choice. Manufacturing locally costs more. We believe it costs less than the alternative.

●

EU production standards

REACH compliance, strict wastewater regulation, and energy efficiency requirements that no offshore alternative meets.

◯

Local hiring in Grasse

We recruit from the community around our atelier. Every role filled locally supports a region that has been at the centre of perfumery for five centuries.

▲

Ethical supply chain

Givaudan Orpur certification covers our upstream suppliers too. Fair pricing, no coercive contracts, and third-party audits at every tier.

◆

No greenwashing

We don't claim carbon neutrality through offset schemes or print vague pledges on our packaging. Every commitment here is operational and verifiable today.

The road ahead

We are working toward
B Corp certification.

B Corp certification is the most rigorous third-party standard for social and environmental performance in business. We are actively working through the assessment, measuring our impact across governance, workers, community, environment, and customers.

We don't have the certification yet. We won't say we do. But every operational decision we make is shaped by the standard we intend to meet, from how we hire to how we source to how we package.

When we certify, it won't be a rebrand. It will simply be confirmation of what we've been doing from the start.
